Ball-Zone Coupling Scheme

The Ball-Zone coupling method is derived from the Bridging Domains proposed by [Xiao2004] and adapted to coupling PFC and FLAC3D by [Frangin2007] and [Breugnot2015]. The reader is referred to the latter, which provides a clear and concise description of the method.
