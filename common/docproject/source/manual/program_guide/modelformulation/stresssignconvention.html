Stress Sign Conventions

There are multiple commonly-used methods of referring to the three principal stress values. Consequently, this is an area that is particularly subject to engendering confusion. It is important to remember at all times that in Itasca codes, positive stresses indicate tension; negative stresses indicate compression.
