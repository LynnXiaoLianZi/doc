block gridpoint list command

Syntax

block gridpoint list keyword <range>

Primary keywords:

apply    density-scaling-factors    displacement    force    joined    mass    position    temperature    velocity    group

List information regarding gridpoints.

apply <keyword>

List boundary condition information by the following keywords.

boundary-elements

List boundary element conditions. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) associated block corner index
(3) associated gridpoint index
(4) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(5) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(6) associated boundary element node
(7) second boundary element node
(8) boundary element load-distribution factor
displacements

List displacement at boundary. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) \(x\)-displacement of boundary corner
(5) \(y\)-displacement of boundary corner
fluid

List displacement at boundary. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) \(x\)-displacement of boundary corner
(5) \(y\)-displacement of boundary corner
free-field
force

forces at boundary. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) total \(x\)-component of force at boundary
(5) total \(y\)-component of force at boundary
(6) \(x\)-component of force added for transient loading
(7) \(x\)-history applied type
(8) \(y\)-component of force added for transient loading
(9) \(y\)-history applied type

Note

The total forces also include the force induced by displacement boundary conditions.

history

List histories* used to apply transient loads. The column headings are:

(1) history index
(2) history type
(3) current value of history

History types are: sine function, cosine function, history record, table, and fish function.

interior <keyword>

List interior boundaries. The keywords are:

displacement

List interior boundary displacements. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) \(x\)-component of displacement at boundary
(5) \(y\)-component of displacement at boundary
force

List interior boundary forces. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) total \(x\)-component of force at boundary
(5) total \(y\)-component of force at boundary
(6) \(x\)-component of force added for transient loading
(7) \(x\)-history type*
(8) \(y\)-component of force added for transient loading
(9) \(y\)-history type*

History types are: sine function, cosine function, history record, table, and fish functions.

history

List histories* used to apply transient loads to interior boundaries. The column headings are:

(1) history index
(2) history type
(3) current value of history

History types are: sine function, cosine function, history record, table, and fish functions.

state

List interior boundary condition state. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) associated block corner index
(3) associated gridpoint index
(4) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(5) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(6) boundary condition in the \(x\)-direction
(7) boundary condition in the \(y\)-direction
(8) boundary condition in the shear direction
(9) boundary condition in the normal direction

Note

The condition numbers for \(x\)-, \(y\)-, normal and shear directions are:

0 free

1 stress (or force)

2 boundary element

3 viscous

4 velocity

velocity

List velocities* at interior boundary corners.

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) \(x\)-velocity of boundary corner
(5) \(x\)-history type*
(6) \(y\)-velocity of boundary corner
(7) \(y\)-history type*

History types are: sine function, cosine function, history record, table, and fish functions.

nonwetting-fluid

List non-wetting fluid pressure and permeability condition. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) permeability conditions at boundary corner:
   0   permeable
   1   impermeable
(5) pore pressure at boundary corner
state

List boundary condition state. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) associated block corner index
(3) associated block index
(4) associated gridpoint index
(5) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(6) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(7) boundary condition in the \(x\)-direction
(8) boundary condition in the \(y\)-direction
(9) free-field boundary condition
(10) boundary condition in the shear direction
(11) boundary condition in the normal direction

Note

The condition numbers for \(x\)-, \(y\)-, normal and shear directions are:

0 free

1 stress (or force)

2 boundary element

3 viscous

4 velocity

The free-field indicator numbers are:

0 free field turned off

1 or 2 free field turned on

thermal

List temperature at boundary. The column headings are:

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) temperature at boundary corner
velocity

List velocities* at boundary corners.

(1) boundary corner index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of boundary corner
(4) \(x\)-velocity of boundary corner
(5) \(x\)-history type*
(6) \(y\)-velocity of boundary corner
(7) \(y\)-history type*

History types are: sine function, cosine function, history record, table, and fish functions.

density-scaling-factors

List gridpoint state data. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) index of linked corner
(3) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(5) gridpoint mass
(6) density scaling factor
displacement

List gridpoint displacements and velocities. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(x\)-velocity of gridpoint
(5) \(y\)-velocity of gridpoint
(6) \(x\)-displacement of gridpoint
(7) \(y\)-displacement of gridpoint
force

List gridpoint forces. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(x\)-direction force at gridpoint
(5) \(y\)-direction force at gridpoint
joined

List all zone gridpoints that are slaved due to the blocks being joined.

mass

List gridpoint state data. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) index of linked corner
(3) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(5) gridpoint mass
(6) density scaling factor
position

List gridpoint state data. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) index of linked corner
(3) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(5) gridpoint mass
(6) density scaling factor
temperature

List gridpoint temperature (see Section 3 in Special Features). The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) temperature at gridpoint
velocity

List gridpoint displacements and velocities. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) \(x\)-velocity of gridpoint
(5) \(y\)-velocity of gridpoint
(6) \(x\)-displacement of gridpoint
(7) \(y\)-displacement of gridpoint
group

List gridpoint group. The column headings are:

(1) gridpoint index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of gridpoint
(4) group name
(5) group slot
