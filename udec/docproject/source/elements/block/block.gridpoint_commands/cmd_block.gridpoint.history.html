block gridpoint history command

Syntax

block gridpoint history keyword <range>

Primary keywords:

acceleration-x    acceleration-y    apply-x    apply-y    displacement-x    displacement-y    velocity-maximum    velocity-x    velocity-y    temperature    unbalanced-force

Get a history of a gridpoint variable.

acceleration-x fx fy

Get \(x\)-acceleration at location nearest to (fx, fy).

acceleration-y fx fy

Get \(y\)-acceleration at location nearest to (fx, fy).

apply-x

Get history of applied \(x\)-force history function.

apply-y

Get history of applied \(y\)-force history function.

displacement-x fx fy

Get \(x\)-displacement at location nearest to (fx, fy).

displacement-y fx fy

Get \(y\)-displacement at location nearest to (fx, fy).

velocity-maximum

Get maximum gridpoint velocity in model.

velocity-x fx fy

Get \(x\)-velocity at location nearest to (fx, fy).

velocity-y fx fy

Get \(y\)-velocity at location nearest to (fx, fy).

temperature fx fy

Get history of temperature of gridpoint nearest to (fx, fy)) (deformable blocks only).

unbalanced-force

Get maximum unbalanced gridpoint force in model.
