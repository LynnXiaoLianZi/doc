gravity

Syntax

Vector Access

v = gravity
gravity = v

Get/set the gravity vector.

Returns:v - gravity vector
Accepts:v - gravity vector

Component Access

f = gravity.x
gravity.x = f

Get/set the \(x\)- component of gravity.

Returns:f - \(x\)- component of gravity
Accepts:f - \(x\)- component of gravity
f = gravity.y
gravity.y = f

Get/set the \(y\)- component of gravity.

Returns:f - \(y\)- component of gravity
Accepts:f - \(y\)- component of gravity
