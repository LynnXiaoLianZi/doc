block contact list command

Syntax

block contact list keyword <range>

Primary keywords:

n    aperture    on-line    barton-bandis    corner    displacement    extra    force    group    hydraulic    model    material-table    neighbor    orientation    property    joint-set    position    radii    state    stiff    stress

List contact data.

n

List all the data for contact index n. Separate parts of this data may be listed using the keywords that follow.

aperture

List current contact hydraulic apertures and aperture for zero normal stress.

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number
(3) joint constitutive number
(4) x-coordinate of contact
(5) y-coordinate of contact
(6) hydraulic aperture
(7) aperture at zero stress (aperture-zero-load)
(8) contact normal displacement
on-line f1 f2 f3 f4 <keyword> <tolerance f >

If no keyword is specified, contact data (e.g., state, displacement and force) will be printed for a single joint along the line from (f1, f2) to (f3, f4) within a band, tolerance f, perpendicular to the line (f1, f2), (f3, f4). The default value for tolerance is twice the corner-round-length. The data are given at distance (R) from joint endpoint (f1, f2).The following keywords can also be given to print specific data for contacts along a single joint.

aperture

List aperture.

flow

List flow rate.

pore-pressure

List domain pressure.

stress-normal

List normal stress.

stress-shear

List shear stress.

velocity

List mean fluid flow velocity.

barton-bandis <keyword>

List joint model contact parameter for Barton-Bandis joints (see Section 3 in Constitutive Models for details).

aperture

List aperture data for Barton-Bandis joints.

normal

List normal direction data for Barton-Bandis joints.

shear

List shear direction data for Barton-Bandis joints.

corner

List corner links. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) contact type:
  1   corner-corner
  2   corner-edge
  3   edge-corner
(5) index of one corner next to contact in one block
(6) index of one corner next to contact in the other block
(7) index of corner linked to contact in one block
(8) index of corner linked to contact in the other block
(9) interpolation factor for contact at one block
(10) interpolation factor for contact at the other block
displacement

List contact displacements. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of contact
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of contact
(4) relative normal displacement of contact
(5) relative shear displacement of contact
extra

This has not been implemented.

force

List contact forces/stresses. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of contact
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of contact
(4) normal stress at contact
(5) shear stress at contact
(6) normal force at contact
(7) shear force at contact
group

List contact group name. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) x-coordinate
(3) y-coordinate
(4) group name
(5) group slot
hydraulic

List hydraulic data. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number (negative if JOINT model)
(3) joint constitutive model number
(4) \(x\)-coordinate of contact
(5) \(y\)-coordinate of contact
(6) normal displacement at contact
(7) hydraulic extension index
information

List contact information

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) x-coordinate
(5) y-coordinate
(6) ratio of shear to normal stress
(7) contact length
(8) contact angle (measured counter-clockwise from positive x axis)
(9) block 1 index
(10) block 2 index
model

List contact model name. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) x-coordinate
(3) y-coordinate
(4) model name
(5) group slot
material-table

Lists table which is used (if defined) to assign properties and models to newly formed contacts based on the property numbers of the blocks in contact.

(1) material number for block 1
(2) material number for block 2
(3) contact material number
(4) contact model
neighbor

List information on neighboring contacts and vertices. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) block 1 index
(3) block 2 index
(4) 1st item for block 1
(5) 2nd item for block 1
(6) next contact on block
(7) 1st item for block 2
(8) 2nd item for block 2
(9) next contact for block 2
orientation

List contact orientation

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) x-coordinate
(5) y-coordinate
(6) ratio of shear to normal stress
(7) contact length
(8) contact angle (measured counter-clockwise from positive x axis)
(9) block 1 index
(10) block 2 index
property s

List the values of named property (s) for each contact in the specified range.

(1) contact index
(2) x-coordinate
(3) y-coordinate
(4) model name
(5) property name (s)
(6) property value
joint-set
(1) contact index
(2) jointset-id
(3) x-coordinate
(4) y-coordinate
(5) block 1 index
(6) block 2 index
position
(1) contact index
(2) joint material number
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) x-coordinate
(5) y-coordinate
(6) ratio of shear to normal stress
(7) contact length
(8) contact angle (measured counter-clockwise from positive x asis)
(9) block 1 index
(10) block 2 index
radii

List corner radii at contact. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number (negative if JOINT model)
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) contact type:
    1  corner-corner
    2  corner-edge
    3  edge-corner
(5) contact overlap
(6) radius of one corner
(7) radius of other corner
(8) distance from contact to one corner center of radius
(9) distance from contact to other corner center of radius
state

List contact state. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) joint material number (negative if JOINT model)
(3) joint constitutive model number (negative if JOINT model)
(4) x-coordinate of contact
(5) y-coordinate of contact
(6) ratio of shear/normal force
(7) contact state
    1  shear failure current
    2  tension failure current
    4  shear failure past
    8  tension failure past
stiff

List information on normal stiffness when applied with the block contact property material stiffness-normal-table command. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) material number
(3) stiffness table number
(4) current joint normal stiffness
(5) maximum normal closure in past.
stress

List contact stresses/forces (If the joint length = 0.0, then the contact force is printed; otherwise, the joint stress is printed.) The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of contact
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of contact
(4) normal stress at contact
(5) shear stress at contact
(6) normal force at contact
(7) shear force at contact
