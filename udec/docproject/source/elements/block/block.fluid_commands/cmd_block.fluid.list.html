block fluid list command

Syntax

block fluid list keyword <range>

Primary keywords:

property    flow    far-field    well

List information related to fluid flow.

property

List fluid flow properties.

flow

List fluid flow data. The column headings are:

(1) contact index
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of contact
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of contact
(4) flow rate across contact
(5) joint length associated with contact
(6) mean aperture at contact
(7) index of domain on one side of contact
(8) index of domain on other side of contact
(9) mean flow (flow rate/aperture)
far-field

List data for the porous medium outer boundary, including a listing of fluid pressures and node coordinates. The column headings are:

(1) grid node number
(2) pressure at node
(3) \(x\)-coordinate of node
(4) \(y\)-coordinate of node
(5) node pressure code:
  0    pressure not fixed
  1    pressure fixed
(6) index of boundary corner linked to node
well

List wells. The column headings are:

(1) domain index of well
(2) \(x\)-coordinate of well
(3) \(y\)-coordinate of well
(4) flow rate at well
