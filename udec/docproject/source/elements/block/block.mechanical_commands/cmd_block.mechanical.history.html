block mechanical history command

Syntax

block mechanical history keyword <range>

Primary keywords:

damp    energy-sum    energy-increment    fragment-energy    solve-local-ratio    solve-ratio    unbalanced-maximum    time-total

Get time histories of mechanical parameters. Available keywords are:

damp

Get current global damping factor.

energy-sum

Get sum of all energy components. The histories of the 20 components of stored and dissipated energy are recorded. (See PRINT energy for a listing of the components.)

energy-increment

Get rate of change in energy components. Histories of the rate of change for 20 energy components are recorded.

fragment-energy

Get sum of kinetic energy in block fragments. Fragments are blocks or groups of blocks with no un-broken connection to the model boundary.

solve-local-ratio

Get the local mechanical force ratio in the model.

solve-ratio

Get the maximum mechanical force ratio in the model.

unbalanced-maximum

Get the maximum unbalanced gridpoint force in the model.

time-total

Get a history of the current mechanincal time.
